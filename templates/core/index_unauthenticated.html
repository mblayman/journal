Welcome to JourneyInbox. JourneyInbox is an online journaling service that works through email. We'll send you a daily prompt directly to your inbox and you update your journal by replying. It's that easy!

Why is that interesting? Because your daily prompt will include a random previous journal entry that can remind you of something you did in your past. This is a great way to reflect on how your life changes over time.

I am still working on the user interface, but the core functionality of interacting with JourneyInbox via email is already there. I am a daily journaler and have been happily using JourneyInbox myself for many months now.
