About

JourneyInbox is a home for your digital journal. This service will email you a daily prompt to remind you to write. Updating your journal is as easy as responding to the email. JourneyInbox will keep track of all of your entries.

Your prompt emails will include a random previous entry to help you recall your past. This is a delightful way to reminisce about your journey through life.

Who Makes This?

JourneyInbox is a hobby project built by me, Matt Layman. I built JourneyInbox for two main reasons.

First, the previous online journal that I was using had somem reliability problems, and I wanted to ensure that I have a service that I can depend upon. If the site is down, there is no one blame but myself!

Second, I am building JourneyInbox as a live code activity on YouTube. If you want to see how this product is built from scratch, you can check out my playlist on YouTube.
